Gentle Baby Skincare Essentials

Confidence from Day 1

QV Baby skincare range consists of gentle hydrating cleansers and moisturisers specially developed for babies' delicate skin. Recommended by paediatricians, every QV Baby product is free from colour, fragrance and other common irritants, so nothing comes between you and your baby.

The QV Baby Bath Oil, Gentle Wash and Cream have the exact same formula you know and love from our original QV Body range. The QV Baby range is designed to make it easier for parents to locate QV Skincare products within the baby section of pharmacies.
